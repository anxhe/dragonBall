How to play?

This is a multiplayer game featuring young Goku and Piccolo, the main purpose is to find the seven dragon balls scattered around the world, but it won't be as easy as there are several enemies wanting to harm you.


Move your player around the grid and try to find the dragon balls. Press "Show Ball" key to attempt to find a dragon ball. The player than finds the greater number of balls will be the winner.

Goku

  • Move up: ⬆️
  • Move dow: ⬇️
  • Move right: ➡️
  • Move left: ⬅️
  • Show ball: SPACE BAR

Piccolo

  • Move up: W
  • Move dow: S
  • Move right: D
  • Move left: A
  • Show ball: SPACE BAR
